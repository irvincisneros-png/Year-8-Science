# Year 8 Science — AI Image Prompts (for Gemini)

These illustrations match the **"playful lab notebook"** look of the Year 7 Forces topic. Generate
them in Gemini, then drop each file into the matching topic folder (paths given per image).

## How to use this doc
1. Paste the **Shared style** block once at the top of your Gemini session (or prepend it to each
   prompt). It keeps every image visually consistent.
2. Then paste an individual **prompt** below it. Prompts are deliberately short — give the model
   creative freedom.
3. Generate at the **exact dimensions** listed. Save with the **filename** given.
4. Transparent PNG where noted ("cutout"); otherwise a soft cream background is fine.

---

## Shared style (paste once, applies to every image)
> Friendly hand-drawn "science lab notebook" illustration. Warm cream paper background (#f7f1e3),
> bold dark-ink outlines (#1b1a17), flat fills, subtle paper texture. Accent palette: teal #0f766e,
> coral #ef6b4b, mustard #d99e1e, sky blue #4b7bc9, plum #7a3a9b, leaf green #3f8a4a. Slightly
> playful and imperfect, like sketched in a notebook. No text, no labels, no watermark.
